About Jacob?

It's a beautiful journey.

Jacob became a fan of origami since very early years and kept exercising until now. He even tried to give paper roses to girls when he was a teenage though that did not work.

Jacob loves reading. He was more into the universe of books instead of real life but this was reversed at a certain time point, maybe some later stage of his PhDhood.

Jacob is a marathon runner, a cyclist, and a swimmer, though he never did sports before 12 years old. It's so funny that a person who loves sports could stay away from his passoion like forever, and one day, all of a sudden, he found it back from the dusty corner of his soul.

Jaocb was dreaming to be a scientist like Steven Hawking, then he turned his goal to a great writer, but later he changed his dream to become a rocket scientist instead. Eventually he finished his PhD in Fluid Mechanics, focusing on Compuational Fluid Mechanics, which is usually called 'Rocket Science' as an idiom.
